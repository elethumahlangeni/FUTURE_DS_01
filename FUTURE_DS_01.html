Key Business Insights
Strategic Recommendations
🎯
Double down on Q4
Plan stock and marketing campaigns from August to capture the full pre-Christmas wholesale demand. Consider early-bird schemes to secure orders in September.
🌐
Expand top international markets
Assign dedicated account managers or local distributors to the top 3 international markets to grow their share beyond 15%.
📦
Protect star SKUs
Ensure top-revenue products are always in stock. Explore bundling them with slower-moving items to improve overall catalogue velocity.
👤
Identify anonymous customers
A significant portion of transactions have no CustomerID. Introducing a login or loyalty scheme would unlock customer lifetime value analysis.
🕐
Stimulate slow months
Revenue dips in Jan–Apr every year. Targeted promotions in this window would smooth cash-flow seasonality across the year.
📊
Build a customer RFM model
Segment customers by Recency, Frequency, and Monetary value to identify top-tier clients and those at risk of churning.
